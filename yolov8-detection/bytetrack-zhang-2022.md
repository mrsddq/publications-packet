# ByteTrack

**Authors**: Zhang et al.
**Year**: 2022
**Link**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2110.06864
**Implementation**: https://github.com/mrsddq/yolov8-detection

## Key Contribution
ByteTrack associates nearly every detection box instead of discarding low-confidence detections too early.

## What I Implemented / Adapted
`scripts/track.py` exposes YOLOv8 tracking with `bytetrack.yaml` for video demos.
