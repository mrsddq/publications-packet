# nnU-Net

**Authors**: Isensee et al.
**Year**: 2021
**Link**: https://arxiv.org/abs/1809.10486
**Implementation**: https://github.com/mrsddq/medical-segmentation

## Key Contribution
nnU-Net shows that careful preprocessing, data-dependent configuration, and postprocessing can outperform many hand-designed segmentation pipelines.

## What I Implemented / Adapted
This portfolio repo does not reimplement nnU-Net. It uses nnU-Net as a reference ceiling and documents why a 2D Attention U-Net is below that level.
